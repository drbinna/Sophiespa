## About Sophie Spa

Sophie Spa is a premium mobile beauty and wellness service based in Auckland, New Zealand.

Sophie travels to clients across Auckland. There is no fixed salon location. All treatments are performed in the client's home, workplace, or hotel suite.

Sophie, also known as Soph, has 19 years of experience in the beauty and wellness industry. She began her journey at age 14 and formally trained in 2007. She holds a CIDESCO diploma and qualified in 2010.

She has postgraduate training in deep tissue massage and experience working with elite professional New Zealand and international rugby teams.

Sophie Spa is operated by one therapist only. This is not a franchise. Every treatment is delivered by Soph personally.

Soph's signature skill is her intuitive ability to locate and release tension. She adapts each treatment in real time based on what your body needs.

Phone: 027 251 6985  
Website: sophiesspa.co.nz  
Location: Mobile service across Auckland, New Zealand
